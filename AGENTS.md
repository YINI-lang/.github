## Content Guidelines

The organization profile should present YINI clearly, factually, and without hype.

Use wording that is:

* Technical.
* Concise.
* Non-salesy.
* Consistent with the current YINI specification and project status.

Do not make unsupported claims about maturity, adoption, benchmarks, ecosystem size, or production usage.

## Links
